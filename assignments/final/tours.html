Tours

Thunder Thighs

Description: Experience the mountains like you never have before with the Thunder Thights tour. This tour is not for amateurs. This tour spends three days going up the mountain and two days going down. Come prepared and get reading to have some burning thighs by the end of the trip.

Pedal Through Petals

Description: This family friendly tour is a easy bike ride that everyone of all skill levels can enjoy. The tour takes a path along the base of the Gallatin Range. Be prepared to see the beautiful nature and amazing wildlife.

Location: Mount Crazy

Length: 1 day

Skill Level Required: Know how to ride bike

Cost: $50 per person or $150 for four
