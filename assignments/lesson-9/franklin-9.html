Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin has seen record breaking amounts of water. This is from a combination of snow melting and then spring rains. Authorities warn people to be careful as they are driving through the back roads. There have already been ten cars that had to be pulled out of deep mud. Please look at the weather and if it has rained a lot, use main roads or wait. It's not worth it. Despite the cars getting stuck, farmers are really excited for the large amounts of water to help their produce grow.
