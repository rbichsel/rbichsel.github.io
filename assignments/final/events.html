Cycle the Continental Divide

The Great Divide Mountain Bike Route is the longest off-pavement route in the world. Bike from the top to the bottom of the United states in 3 weeks. This is a bike ride that all bike enthusiasts need to experience. There's no better way to tour this beautiful country.

Dates: July 15 - August 4, 2018

Route: Banff, Alberta, Cananda to Antelope Wells, NM

Costs: $700

Requirements: Must provide own bike. Bring tire repair exquipmen.
